UIL Number Sense - 25's Trick

"The trick to multiplying by 25 is to think of it as 100/4.
So the strategy is to take what ever you are multiplying with, divide it by 4 then move the decimal over to the right two places"
Dividing is similar, but takes the opposite steps. Think of 4/100.
So multiply by 4, and move the decimal to the left 2 places.
To remember: use the opposite sign. If your multiplying obviously the answer will get bigger, hence going to the right. Dividing will get smaller, so the decimal would move to the left.
